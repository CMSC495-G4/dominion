Rules of Dominion

Action Phase

At the start of their turn, a player may play an action card currently in their hand. If another player has a valid reaction card, they may respond to the player's action(s).

Buy Phase

After all actions have been completed, the player may then purchase a card from the supply using coins from treasure cards. The purchased card is then placed in the discard pile.

Cleanup Phase

At the end of their turn, the player places any remaining cards in the discard pile and draws five new cards. If there aren't enough cards, the discard pile is shuffled into the player's deck.
